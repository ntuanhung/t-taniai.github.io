A List of Computer Vision and Image Processing Conferences

This list was created by Tatsunori Taniai for his personal interest. The sources of the information include official announcements, personal reports, and other unofficial records on the web. I welcome contributers to maintain the table contents by sending pull requests! (The table is formatted by tab-separated values.)

Sorting: Multi-column sorting function is available by clicking header columns with the shift key

Name: the conference name
Year: the year of the conference
Date: the begining date of the conference (or workshops/tutorials prior to the main conference)
Days: the days of the main conference and workshops/tutorials
Deadline: the final deadline date (after extension if it was extended)
#Papers: the number of accepted papers
#Orals: the numer of oral papers
#Submitted: the number of total submissions (may or may not contain invalid submissions depending on official announcements)
Total AR: the overall acceptance rate (#Papers / #Submitted)
Oral AR: the oral acceptance rate (#Orals / #Submitted)
Place: the conference place
Note: additional information e.g. original deadline

ICCV: International Confefence on Computer Vision. Held in odd years. Can submit after CVPR notifications. 6-8 pages in double column excluding references (ICCV 2015).
CVPR: IEEE Confefence on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition. 6-8 pages in double column excluding references (CVPR 2015).
ECCV: European Confefence on Computer Vision. Held in even years. Can submit after CVPR notifications. 14 pages in single column excluding references (ECCV 2014).
ACCV: Asian Confefence on Computer Vision. Held in even years. Can submit after ECCV notifications. 14 pages in single column excluding references (ACCV 2014).
BMVC: British Machine Vision Conference. 9 pages in single column excluding references (BMVC 2015).
ICPR: International Confefence on Pattern Recognition. Held in even years. 6 pages in double column including references (ICPR 2014).
ACPR: Asian Confefence on Pattern Recognition. Held in odd years. 5 pages in double column including references (ACPR 2013).
EMMCVPR: International Conference on Energy Minimization Methods in Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition. Held in odd years. 14 pages in single column including references (EMMCVPR 2015).
